About CashFlow

CashFlow (CFS) is a decentralized Solana-based community token that started as a pump.fun experiment — but aims to grow into something more meaningful: a playful, transparent ecosystem powered by its holders.

The project values transparency (mint address and liquidity status are public), fair access (no hidden taxes or mint authority abuse), and community involvement through social events, polls, and community-led initiatives.

Whitepaper (Summary)

CashFlow (CFS) is designed as an open, community-first token experiment. Its purpose is to explore how collective attention, humor, and transparency can drive token value without traditional funding. The tokenomics model ensures fairness by locking liquidity and distributing a majority of supply to the community.

The long-term vision is to transform the pump.fun origin into a self-sustaining ecosystem — with a DAO-controlled treasury and ongoing community incentives. Every holder contributes to governance, event voting, and roadmap decisions.

Mission: Build an honest, transparent, and fun experiment that proves crypto can be social, simple, and sustainable.
